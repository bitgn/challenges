# ECOM1 Code of Conduct
**BitGN Agent Challenge: E-commerce** (ECOM1)  
**Audience:** everyone (participants + hubs + community)  
**Version:** 0.1  
**Last updated:** 5 May 2026 (Vienna time)

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- ECOM1 online spaces operated or moderated by BitGN
- ECOM1-branded community activity
- hub events using ECOM1 branding

## Expected behavior

- be respectful and collaborative
- critique ideas, not people
- respect privacy and confidentiality
- follow venue rules and local laws
- report security or integrity concerns responsibly

## Prohibited behavior

- harassment, discrimination, hate speech
- doxxing, intimidation, threats
- sexual harassment or unwanted sexual attention
- deliberate disruption of events or community spaces
- attempts to exploit platform vulnerabilities or other participants' systems
- sharing sensitive participant, customer-like, or benchmark data outside allowed channels

## Safety & legality

- no unsafe instructions for real-world harm
- comply with venue policies and local laws
- do not use ECOM1 materials to encourage real-world fraud, payment abuse, or privacy violations
- do not present local community activity as globally sponsored or officially hosted by BitGN unless BitGN has explicitly approved that wording

## Reporting

Report violations to the organizer or support contact. Provide:
- what happened, when, and where
- who was involved
- screenshots, logs, or links if available

## Enforcement

Possible actions include:
- warning
- removal from online spaces
- removal from hub listing eligibility
- leaderboard disqualification for integrity-related violations
- platform access restrictions for severe violations

## Hub addendum

Hubs may extend this Code of Conduct with stricter local rules, but **cannot weaken it**.
