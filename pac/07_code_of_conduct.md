# BitGN PAC Code of Conduct
**BitGN Agent Challenge: Personal & Trustworthy** (BitGN PAC)  
**Audience:** everyone (participants + hubs + community)  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** 19 Feb 2026 (Vienna time)

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- BitGN PAC online spaces (platform and official community surfaces)
- hub events using BitGN PAC branding

## Expected behavior
- be respectful and collaborative
- critique ideas, not people
- respect privacy and confidentiality
- follow venue rules and local laws

## Prohibited behavior
- harassment, discrimination, hate speech
- doxxing, intimidation, threats
- sexual harassment or unwanted sexual attention
- deliberate disruption of events or community spaces

## Safety & legality
- no unsafe instructions for real-world harm
- comply with venue policies and local laws
- do not exploit platform vulnerabilities or other participantsâ€™ systems

## Reporting
Report violations to the organizer/support contact. Provide:
- what happened, when and where
- who was involved
- screenshots/logs if available

## Enforcement
Possible actions include:
- warning
- removal from online spaces
- removal from hub listing eligibility
- leaderboard disqualification (integrity-related)
- platform access restrictions for severe violations

## Hub addendum
Hubs may extend this Code of Conduct with stricter rules, but **cannot weaken it**.
